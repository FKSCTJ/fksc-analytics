◈ Setup Quality A/B/C

How strong was your analytical edge? A = high confluence, multiple confirmations aligned. B = moderate confluence, 1–2 factors missing. C = weaker setup, low confluence, avoid repeating.

◉ Execution Quality 1–3

How clean was the execution? 3 = clean, 2 = decent, 1 = poor. Catches the classic mistake of rushing entries on low-conviction trades.

◇ Management 1–5

How well did you manage the trade? Based on R-multiple outcome and exit behaviour. Trailing stops and riding winners score high. Cutting early or moving stops against you scores low.
